UNION

The UNION operator joins the outputs of two or more queries (SELECT statements) into single set of rows and columns having distinct records.

SYNTAX

            SELECT Column_list from Table1
            UNION
            SELECT Column_list from Table2
            [ORDER BY CLAUSE];
Consider the following table 'EMP':
DEPTNO ENAME JOB
10 CLARK MANAGER
20 SMITH CLERK
20 JONES MANAGER
30 ALLEN SALESMAN
30 JAMES CLERK
To display the different designations in department 20 and 30 of 'EMP' table: 
SELECT JOB FROM EMP
WHERE DEPTNO = 20
UNION
SELECT JOB FROM EMP
WHERE DEPTNO = 30;
OUTPUT:

JOB
CLERK
MANAGER
SALESMAN
