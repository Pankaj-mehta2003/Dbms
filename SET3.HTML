INTERSECT

The INTERSECT operator returns the rows that are common between two sets of rows. INTERSECT operator, like UNION operator, removes all duplicated rows from the result set.

SYNTAX

SELECT Column_list from Table1
INTERSECT
SELECT Column_list from Table2
[ORDER BY CLAUSE];
Consider the following table 'EMP':
DEPTNO ENAME JOB
10 CLARK MANAGER
20 SMITH CLERK
20 JONES MANAGER
30 ALLEN SALESMAN
30 JAMES CLERK
To display the jobs common in department 20 and 30 of 'EMP' table: 
SELECT JOB FROM EMP
WHERE DEPTNO = 20
INTERSECT
SELECT JOB FROM EMP
WHERE DEPTNO = 30;
OUTPUT:

JOB
CLERK
